############################ READ ME ####################################
Selected stellar calibrator candidates for ESA's Ariel space mission
By Elena Tonucci (Leiden Observatory, SRON, TU Delft), 2024
#########################################################################

This file describes the columns in Selected_stellar_calibrators_list.txt with their units, a catalogue containing the stars passing all three selection criteria from Tonucci et al. 2024. Currently, the most promising stellar calibrators for ESA's Ariel's space mission.

Column descriptions:
0. TIC: TESS Input Catalog identifier. String.
1. RA: Right ascension in ICRS at the epoch J2000.0. Unit: [°]
2. Dec: Declination in ICRS at the epoch J2000.0. Unit: [°]
4. sigma_Mdn_ppm: Dispersion around the median of the light curve time series observation in a normalised unit. Unit: [ppm]
